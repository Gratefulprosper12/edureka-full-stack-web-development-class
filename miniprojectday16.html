What Is Netflix

Netflix is a streaming service that offers movies, TV shows, documentaries, and more. You can watch Netflix on many devices, including phones, tablets, laptops, and TVs.
Netflix offers multiple subscription plans, including Standard with Ads, Standard, and Premium. The main difference between the plans is the number of devices you can stream on simultaneously, and whether the plan includes ads.
